| Metric | What It Means | Good Range | Red Flag |
|---|---|---|---|

| Debt/Equity | How much debt vs own money. | Below 1 for most industries | Above 2 (too much debt) |
| Revenue Growth | Is the company growing? | Above 10% year-on-year | Negative growth for 2+ years |
| Promoter Holding | Do the owners believe in their own company? | Above 50% | Declining every quarter |

## B.6 Traps That Destroy Beginners

| Trap | What Happens | How to Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| Trading based on YouTube tips | "XYZ stock will 10x!" You buy at peak, it crashes 50% | NEVER buy based on anyone's tip. Do your own analysis. |
| Day trading / F&O | "I'll make ₹10K/day trading options" — 93% of F&O traders LOSE money (SEBI data) | DO NOT touch Futures & Options for at least 3 years |
| Penny stocks | "₹2 stock will become ₹200!" — Most penny stocks go to ₹0 | Don't buy stocks below ₹100 for the first 3 years |
| Crypto as "investment" | "Bitcoin will 100x!" — It might, or it might drop 80% | Maximum 5% of your portfolio in crypto. Only money you can lose 100%. |
| Checking portfolio daily | See -5% drop, panic sell. See +10% gain, get overconfident. | Check once a month. Set it and forget it. |
| Borrowing to invest | "I'll take loan at 12% and invest at 15%" — works until it doesn't | NEVER invest borrowed money. Only invest what you've earned and saved. |
| Timing the market | "I'll buy when market is low" — you'll wait forever | SIP automatically buys at all levels. Time IN market > timing THE market. |

**The single most important rule:** 93% of F&O (Futures & Options) traders lose money. This is SEBI's official data, not opinion. If someone tells you to trade options, they are telling you to gamble. Don't do it until you have 5+ years of investing experience and money you can afford to lose.
