# The Problem — What Is Actually Breaking Right Now

## The Certificate Chain Is Broken

```
HOW IT SHOULD WORK:
Household gives old phone → Collection center weighs and records
→ Material sent to recycler with documentation
→ Recycler processes and generates certificate
→ Certificate sent to PRO
→ PRO bundles and sends to Brand
→ Brand submits to CPCB
→ CPCB verifies and marks brand compliant

HOW IT ACTUALLY WORKS TODAY:
Household gives old phone → Nobody records anything
→ Material moves informally through kabadiwala chain
→ Somewhere a certificate gets generated
→ Numbers are inflated to fill targets
→ PRO buys floating certificates from market
→ Brand submits questionable certificates to CPCB
→ CPCB is starting to catch this
```

## What The Platform Does

### Layer 1 — Collection Point Verification
```
Staff at collection center opens your app on phone
Scans or enters incoming device details
Takes photo of material
Weighs on digital scale (app records weight)
GPS location auto tagged
Timestamp automatic
Unique batch QR code generated

This data:
→ Cannot be backdated
→ Cannot be faked
→ Flows instantly to PRO dashboard
→ Creates beginning of verified chain
```

### Layer 2 — Material Movement Tracking
```
Every kg has a digital chain of custody

Collection center → PRO warehouse:
Driver scans batch QR on pickup
PRO receives notification
Weight verified at warehouse
Chain of custody updated

PRO warehouse → Recycler facility:
Recycler scans incoming batch
Weight recorded
Material type confirmed
Chain of custody extended
```

### Layer 3 — Recycler Processing Records
```
Recycler opens app
Scans incoming batch QR
Records processing:
→ Input weight confirmed
→ Processing method
→ Output weight per material type
→ Recovery rate calculated automatically
→ Photos of processing

System generates certificate:
→ Unique QR code
→ Links to original collection point
→ Shows complete chain of custody
→ CPCB format compliant
→ Cannot be duplicated
→ Cannot be inflated
```
