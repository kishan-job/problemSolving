# PATH 1: Tech Person → AI Empire

```
TOTAL JOURNEY: ₹0 → ₹100Cr+ company in 10-15 years
STARTING REQUIREMENT: Laptop + willingness to learn AI/coding
```

---

## PHASE 1: AI Freelance Agency (Month 1–12)

**What you do:** Build AI solutions (chatbots, automation, document processing) for US/EU small businesses. Sell in USD, build in INR. Start alone, grow to 3-5 person team.

**Month 1-2: Learn + Build Demo**
```
Week 1:   Learn LangChain or OpenAI API (free YouTube courses, 2 hours/day)
Week 2:   Build ONE demo — AI chatbot for a specific industry (dental clinics,
          e-commerce, real estate). Record 3-minute Loom walkthrough.
Week 3:   Set up LinkedIn profile as specialist. NOT "AI Developer."
          YES "I Build AI Customer Support Bots for Shopify Stores."
Week 4:   Start cold outreach. 30 personalized LinkedIn messages per day
          to US Shopify store owners. Share your demo video.
```

**Month 3-4: First Clients**
```
From ~600 messages, expect: 30 replies → 10 calls → 2-3 clients.
First client: Charge $1,500 (discounted) for a video testimonial.
Second client: $2,500 (closer to full rate).
Deliver EXCEPTIONALLY. Overdeliver. Client success = your reputation.
```

**Month 5-12: Scale to $10,000-20,000/month**
```
Month 5-6:   5 clients at $2,000-4,000/month each.
             Revenue: $10,000-20,000/month (₹8-16L/month).
Month 7-8:   Hire first developer (₹40-60K/month Indian salary).
             You focus on sales + client management.
Month 9-12:  8-10 clients. Revenue: $20,000-40,000/month (₹16-33L/month).
             Hire 2nd developer. Systematize delivery into templates.
             Save ₹20-30L for Phase 2 investment.
```

**What you're learning (for the empire):**
- Which AI problems businesses ACTUALLY pay for (not what's cool in research)
- How businesses operate (their workflows, pain points, data)
- How to sell, deliver, and retain clients
- How to manage a technical team

With proven revenue + growth rate, raise ₹5-20Cr from angels/VCs.

## PHASE 1: AMC + Scrap Trading Business (Month 1–18)

**What you do:** Start with the safest business from the previous guide (AMC/Maintenance) to generate immediate cashflow. SIMULTANEOUSLY start scrap metal trading on the side — learning materials, markets, and the waste-to-value chain.

**Month 1-6: AMC Business for Cashflow**
```
Month 1:    Start water purifier AMC business (from Rank #1 guide).
            Visit apartment societies. Sign 50 AMC customers.
            Revenue: ₹50 × ₹2,499 = ₹1.25L collected upfront.
Month 2-3:  Scale to 150 customers. Hire 1 technician.
            Revenue: ₹3-4L collected. You're earning, bills are paid.
Month 4-6:  300+ AMC customers. Revenue: ₹7-8L/year. Business runs itself
            with 1-2 technicians. You spend 2-3 hours/day managing it.
            REST of your time → Phase 1B.
```

**Month 3-18: Scrap Trading (Learning the Material Business)**
```
Month 3-4:  Visit your city's scrap market (Mayapuri in Delhi, Dharavi in
            Mumbai, every city has one). OBSERVE. Talk to kabadiwallas.
            Learn: which metals exist, what they cost, who buys them.
            Buy ₹10,000 worth of sorted copper scrap. Sell to recycler.
            Make ₹1,000-2,000 profit. LEARN the margins.

Month 5-8:  Start buying mixed electronic scrap (old computers, phones).
            Learn to identify: copper wire, aluminum heatsinks, steel frames,
            circuit boards (most valuable), batteries.
            Sort and sell separately. Sorted scrap = 3-5x mixed scrap price.
            Revenue: ₹50,000-1,00,000/month from scrap trading.

Month 9-12: Specialize in e-waste. Visit IT companies and offices.
            Offer FREE pickup of old electronics (they pay YOU to remove
            their e-waste headache). Sort → sell to authorized recyclers.
            Revenue: ₹1-3L/month. Now you know the material, the market,
            and the regulatory landscape.

Month 12-18: Get SPCB authorization for e-waste collection.
             Start providing disposal CERTIFICATES to companies (EPR compliance).
             Approach Samsung, HP, Dell for EPR partnerships.
             Revenue: ₹3-5L/month from e-waste + AMC combined.
```

**What you're learning (for the empire):**
- What materials are in electronic waste and their value
- The recycling supply chain — who buys what, at what price
- Regulatory landscape — CPCB, SPCB, hazardous waste rules
- How metals are processed (by visiting recyclers you sell to)
- Business relationships in the metal/recycling industry

         Add lithium-ion as EV batteries start retiring.

Revenue: ₹20-50L/month.
Apply: PLI scheme for Advanced Chemistry Cells.
